Trent Ltd - Intrinsic Valuation

Come on! Valuations don't make sense, isn't it? They can get absolutely wrong!

My favorite penny stock 'Robinhood' seemed like a bargain (at $40) before it went penny. With a drop of tear running down my cheeks, here I present to you an Intrinsic Valuation of Trent Ltd.

Trent Ltd has a lifestyle retail business of apparels, footwear, accessories, toys and games. It also runs a Supermarket Grocery Chain in a 50/50 joint venture.

Westside (200 stores) - It offers branded fashion apparel, footwear and accessories for women, men and children, along with a wide range of home furnishings & decor.

Zudio (233 stores) - It is the company's value fashion concept and offers fashion at competitive prices for women, men and children.

Star Market (70 stores) - It is a hypermarket and supermarket store chain and offers various products including staples, beverages, health & beauty products, vegetables, fruits, dairy and non-vegetarian products, cleaning-aids, home care products, packaged foods, beverages and personal care products.

Zara & Massima Dutti (24 stores) - The company has 2 separate associations with Inditex Group wherein it holds 49% stake. One entity is engaged in operating 21 stores of Zara and other entity is engaged in operating 3 stores of Massimo Dutti in India.

Other Concepts (19 stores) - The other concepts of the co. with a smaller footprint includes Booker Wholesale, Value Mandi, Landmark and Utsa.
